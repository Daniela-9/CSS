Melissani Lake

The Melissani Cave is located about 2km from the Sami village on the island of Kefalonia.

The cave lake is one of the most popular tourist attractions on the island.

With its two chambers,one dark and covered with stalagmites and the other sunlit through a huge opening to the surface.

The area only opened to the public in the early 1960s, with plenty of travelers rowing through its stunning blue water since then.
